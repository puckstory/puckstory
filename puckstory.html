Puckstory

Puckstory is an interactive network graph of every player engraved on the Stanley Cup from 1915 to 2026, and the champion teams they won with. It covers 1306 players, 110 champions, and 2307 engravings. The graph is drawn on a canvas; this text version lists the same data so screen readers and search engines can read it.
